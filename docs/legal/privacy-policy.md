# Privacy Policy — Tripvia

*Last updated: 2026-07-20*

## 1. Who we are

Tripvia is a mileage tracking mobile application for individuals and
teams to easily track and report their business trips for tax purposes,
operated by **Weblio s. r. o.**, Jilemnického 1142/12, 040 01 Košice, Slovakia, IČO 57 696 497
(weblioapp.com). For privacy questions contact **info@weblioapp.com**.

## 2. What data we collect

We collect only the data you explicitly provide or that is required for the
app to function:

- Email address and password (for authentication via Supabase)
- Full name, company name, Tax ID (optional, for report headers)
- Vehicle details (name, plate number, fuel type, odometer)
- Trip data: start/end addresses, distance, date, purpose
- GPS location data when background tracking is enabled
- Subscription and purchase status (plan, billing period, renewal date) if
  you subscribe
- Crash and performance diagnostics (device type, OS version, app errors)

## 3. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the trip logging, reporting, and
subscription features of the app. We do not sell, share, or use your data
for advertising or profiling. Trip data is used to generate tax reports at
your explicit request.

## 4. Location data

Background location tracking is optional and must be explicitly enabled in
the app settings. While active, a persistent notification shows tracking is
running (Android foreground service requirement). Location data is used
only for automatic trip detection. Raw GPS coordinates are stored
temporarily and then processed into trip records. You can disable tracking
at any time in Settings.

## 5. Subscriptions and payment data

Paid plans are billed and processed entirely by Google Play (Android) or
the App Store (iOS) — we never see or store your payment card details. We
use RevenueCat to manage subscription status across devices; RevenueCat
receives your purchase receipt and a pseudonymous account identifier (your
Tripvia user ID) from the store, and forwards your active plan and
renewal date to us so the app can unlock the features you paid for.

## 6. Data storage

Data is stored securely on Supabase (supabase.com) servers in the EU
region. All connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Each user can only
access their own data (Row Level Security enforced at database level).

## 7. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you have an active account. You can
delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting
info@weblioapp.com. Generated reports stored in cloud storage are also
deleted upon account deletion.

## 8. Your rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU/EEA you have the right to: access your personal data,
correct inaccurate data, request erasure, restrict processing, and data
portability. To exercise these rights contact info@weblioapp.com.

## 9. Third-party services

- Supabase (database, auth, storage) — supabase.com/privacy
- Google Maps / Places API (address search, routing) — policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Takeout data processed locally and on our servers only when you
  initiate an import
- RevenueCat (subscription management) — revenuecat.com/privacy
- Google Play / App Store (payment processing) — respective store privacy
  policies
- Sentry (crash and error diagnostics) — sentry.io/privacy

## 10. Children

This app is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly
collect data from children.

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The current version is always available in the
app under Settings → Privacy Policy, and at this URL. Significant changes
will be communicated via the app.
